Comparative Study: Do longer Sessions mean better student performance?

Summary and Contents

Key Report Takeaways:

  • Texas writing students consistently have slightly lower understanding ratings compared to other students across the nation
  • A high proportion of students enrolled in ESL courses have lwoer understanding ratings despite having on average more sessions.
  • Texas students in particular request a lot of help forming thesis statements for essays.

Target groups for this study:

  • Texas Community Colleges
  • Texas Colleges with asynchronous writing tutoring
  • Texas College Writing Centers with Large Workloads
